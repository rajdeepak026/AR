Our Story

Founded in 2025 by Raj Deepak, Armaan Ali, and Adarsh, ApkaDr was born out of a shared vision to make quality healthcare accessible to everyone in Saran and across India. After witnessing the challenges patients face in booking appointments and accessing doctors in rural areas, we set out to create a solution that bridges the gap between healthcare providers and those who need care. Today, we're proud to serve thousands of patients and continue innovating to improve healthcare access.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ApkaDr

Is ApkaDr free to use?

Yes, ApkaDr is completely free for patients to use. You can search for doctors, view profiles, and book appointments without any charges. Consultation fees are paid directly to the doctor according to their rates.

How do I know the doctors are qualified?

Every doctor on ApkaDr undergoes a rigorous verification process where we check their medical degrees, licenses, and certifications. We also verify their experience and specialization before onboarding them to our platform.

Can I cancel or reschedule an appointment?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule appointments through your ApkaDr account. Please note that cancellation policies may vary by doctor. We recommend making changes at least 24 hours before your scheduled appointment to avoid any cancellation fees that some doctors may charge.

Who are the founders of ApkaDr?

ApkaDr was founded in 2025 by Raj Deepak (IIT Madras alumnus), Armaan Ali, and Adarsh. The team combines technical expertise, healthcare knowledge, and business strategy to make quality healthcare accessible to everyone in India.

Is my personal health information secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption to protect all your data. Your health information is only shared with the doctors you choose to consult with, and we never sell or share your data with third parties without your consent.
