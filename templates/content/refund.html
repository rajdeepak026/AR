Refund Policy

Effective Date: June 14, 2025

At ApnaDr, we are committed to providing a smooth and transparent experience for patients booking appointments. Please read our refund policy carefully before using our platform.

1. No Refund Policy

Once an appointment is booked and the payment is completed, the fee is non-refundable, even if you are unable to attend the appointment or cancel it later. This policy is in place to maintain fairness to doctors and ensure service continuity.

2. Missed Appointments

If you miss your scheduled appointment or fail to appear at the designated time, no refund will be issued. We advise users to arrive on time and verify their availability before booking.

3. Doctor Cancellations

If a doctor cancels or is unavailable at the scheduled time, we will make every effort to reschedule your appointment. If rescheduling is not possible, a full refund may be issued at our discretion.

4. Technical Issues

In rare cases where payment is deducted but the booking does not confirm, please contact our support team within 24 hours. If verified, we will refund the full amount to the original payment method.

5. Contact for Refund Requests

For refund-related concerns, please contact us with booking details:

  • Email: support@apnadr.in

6. Changes to This Policy

ApnaDr reserves the right to modify this refund policy at any time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

By booking an appointment through ApnaDr, you agree to this Refund Policy.
