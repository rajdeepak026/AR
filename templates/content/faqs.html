Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is ApnaDr.?

ApnaDr. is a user-friendly online platform that allows patients to search for doctors and book appointments online. Whether you're looking for a general physician, dentist, dermatologist, or specialist, ApnaDr. makes it easy to connect with verified medical professionals near you.

2. How do I book a doctor appointment?

Booking a doctor appointment is simple. Just search for a doctor based on specialty and location, select your preferred doctor and time slot, and confirm the appointment. You’ll get instant confirmation via SMS or email.

3. Is it free to book appointments on ApnaDr.?

Yes, we charge a minimum fee per appointment on ApnaDr. This small service charge helps us maintain the platform and ensure a smooth booking experience for both patients and doctors. The exact fee will be shown clearly before you confirm your booking, with no hidden charges.

4. Can I book online doctor consultations?

Currently, ApnaDr. supports only in-person doctor appointment bookings. We do not offer online video consultations at this time. You can easily find and book appointments with nearby verified doctors through our platform.

5. How do I know the doctors listed are genuine?

All doctors on ApnaDr. are verified by our team. We check their qualifications, licenses, and registrations before listing them on the platform.

6. Can I cancel or reschedule an appointment?

No, cancellations are not allowed once an appointment is booked on ApnaDr. If a cancellation is made, the booking amount is non-refundable. Please ensure that you are certain before confirming your appointment.

7. Will I receive reminders for my appointment?

Yes, ApnaDr. provides in-app notifications to help you stay updated about your appointments. You can also check your booking progress and status directly through the app. SMS and email reminders are not available currently.

8. How do I find the best doctors near me?

You can search doctors based on city, area, or specialty. You can also filter by ratings, availability, or consultation type (in-person/online).

9. Do I need to sign up to use ApnaDr.?

You can browse doctors without an account, but booking and managing appointments requires a quick sign-up for security and notification purposes.

10. In which cities is ApnaDr. available?

Currently, ApnaDr. is expanding its services in local areas of Saran. We are working to bring verified doctors from nearby towns and localities to the platform. Please enter your location to check for available appointments near you.

11. What types of doctors are available on ApnaDr.?

ApnaDr. connects you with a wide range of specialists including general physicians, pediatricians, gynecologists, dermatologists, dentists, orthopedics, and more.

12. Can I see reviews and ratings of doctors?

Yes, you can read authentic patient reviews and see star ratings on each doctor’s profile before booking your appointment.

13. Is my personal data secure with ApnaDr.?

We prioritize data security and privacy. All your information is encrypted and shared only with the concerned doctor. We do not sell your data to any third party.

14. How do I contact ApnaDr. support?

You can reach our customer support team via the “Contact Us” page, or email us directly at support@apnadr.com. We’re here to help you 24/7.
