Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 14, 2025

At ApnaDr, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our platform to book doctor appointments.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, email, and address when you sign up or book an appointment.
  • Booking Information: Doctor selection, appointment date/time, and location.
  • Device & Log Information: IP address, device type, browser type, and usage activity.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To schedule and confirm appointments with doctors.
  • To send appointment-related notifications through the app.
  • To improve our services and user experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share it with:

  • Verified doctors you choose to book with.
  • Trusted third-party services for technical and operational purposes (e.g., payment gateways, hosting).
  • Authorities if required by law.

4. Data Security

We use secure technologies and best practices to protect your information. However, no system can be 100% secure, and we encourage you to use strong passwords and update them regularly.

5. Your Choices

  • You can update your information in your user profile.
  • You may request deletion of your account by contacting us at support@apnadr.in.

6. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
